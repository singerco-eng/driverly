# DR-003: Driver Vehicle Management

> **Last Updated:** 2026-01-16  
> **Status:** Draft  
> **Phase:** 3 - Driver Core

---

## Overview

Driver Vehicle Management allows drivers to view and manage their vehicles. For **1099 drivers**, this means full control over their owned vehicles. For **W2 drivers**, this means viewing assigned company vehicles with limited editing (photos only).

**Key Principle:** 1099 drivers own their vehicles and have full management capability. W2 drivers have company vehicles assigned to them and can only update photos.

## User Stories

### 1099 Driver Stories

1. **As a 1099 Driver**, I want to add a vehicle so that I can use it for trips.

2. **As a 1099 Driver**, I want to edit my vehicle information to keep it current.

3. **As a 1099 Driver**, I want to set my primary vehicle for default trip assignments.

4. **As a 1099 Driver**, I want to see which of my vehicles is complete and eligible.

5. **As a 1099 Driver**, I want to set a vehicle inactive when it's not available.

6. **As a 1099 Driver**, I want to retire a vehicle I no longer use.

7. **As a 1099 Driver**, I want to see credential status for each vehicle.

8. **As a 1099 Driver**, I want to see which brokers my vehicle is eligible for.

### W2 Driver Stories

9. **As a W2 Driver**, I want to see my assigned company vehicle(s).

10. **As a W2 Driver**, I want to view the details of my assigned vehicle.

11. **As a W2 Driver**, I want to update photos on my assigned vehicle.

12. **As a W2 Driver**, I want to see vehicle credentials for my assigned vehicle.

## Business Rules

1. **1099 full control:** 1099 drivers can add, edit, and retire their own vehicles

2. **W2 photos only:** W2 drivers can only update photos on assigned vehicles

3. **Vehicle completion:** All required fields must be filled for vehicle to be "complete"

4. **Complete for trips:** Cannot take trips with incomplete vehicle

5. **Primary required:** Must always have a primary vehicle (auto-set if only one)

6. **Type change impact:** Changing vehicle type may add/remove credential requirements

7. **Retire archives:** Retiring a vehicle archives its credentials (not deleted)

8. **Eligibility display:** Show broker eligibility to help driver understand their options

9. **Photo guidelines:** Show tips for good photos during capture

10. **No vehicle limit:** Drivers can have unlimited vehicles
